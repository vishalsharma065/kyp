This program is targeted at all aspirants in the age group of 15-28 years (Age limit for SC/ST, OBC & People with Disabilities is as follows: SC/ST - 33 years, OBC - 31 years, PwD - 33 years), who have passed at least 10th Class irrespective of their having attained higher education or their currently pursuing higher education.

Through this program, BSDM intends to enhance the employability of these youth of Bihar.

  • We offer training in life skills, communication, and basic computer literacy.
  • We offer training in speaking, listening, understanding, reading, and writing in English and Hindi.
  • We offer training in computer basics, including:
    • Using the mouse and keyboard
    • Understanding input, process, and output
    • Computer hardware and software
  • We help people improve their skills to be more employable in today's job market.
  • We help people prepare for jobs, higher education, or entrepreneurial ventures.

Basic Computer Skills

  • Introduction to computers and their uses
  • Operating a computer: Turning it on/off, logging in, and navigating the desktop
  • Using a mouse and keyboard efficiently

Microsoft Office Applications

  • MS Word: Creating, formatting, and saving documents
  • MS Excel: Basic spreadsheet functions, data entry, and simple calculations
  • MS PowerPoint: Creating slides, adding text/images, and making presentations

Job Readiness Training

  • Resume Writing: How to create a professional CV
  • Job Applications: Applying for jobs online and offline
  • Interview Skills: How to present yourself and answer questions confidently

Workplace Etiquette & Communication

  • Professional behavior in the workplace
  • Basic business communication and teamwork skills

Language & Literacy Training

  • Hindi & English Communication: Speaking, reading, and writing basics
  • Improving confidence in communication for better job opportunities

Soft Skills for a Better Future

  • Time management and discipline
  • Building confidence and a positive attitude
  • Personal grooming and professional conduct

Age Criteria: Candidates aged 15-28 years are eligible. Age limit extensions: SC/ST - 33 years, OBC - 31 years, PwD - 33 years.

Educational Qualification: Minimum qualification required is 10th pass. Candidates can apply regardless of whether they are currently pursuing or have completed any higher education course.

Mandatory Training: Youth aged 20-25 years receiving self-help allowance must complete training under the "Kushal Yuva Program."
